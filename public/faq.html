Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click the user icon in the header and fill out the sign up form with your details.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, and PayPal for secure payments.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking link via email and in your account dashboard.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes, we offer free shipping on orders over $75.
Can I return or exchange products?
Absolutely! You can return or exchange products within 14 days of delivery. Please see our return policy for details.
How do I contact customer support?
Visit our Contact page or email us at support@evercart.com. We're here to help!
